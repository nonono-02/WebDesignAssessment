Playable Characters

Five Climbers, Five Fates

← Back to Home

Each character carries a distinct pool of cards and a personal relic that warps the run from the first floor. Experimentation across their kits reveals the Spire's deepest secrets.

The Ironclad

The soldier returns with burning blood and a reworked arsenal. Sacrifices health for strength, leaning into exhaust synergies and devastating self-damage combos.

The Silent

A huntress who thrives on precision. New shiv and poison chains allow her to cycle through the deck at lethal speed, stripping enemy intent layer by layer.

The Regent

A noble draped in dark finery. Commands spectral blades that hover between card slots, converting unplayed energy into delayed, catastrophic strikes.

The Necrobinder

A mage who binds fallen minions into temporary cards. Summons grow stronger the longer a fight drags on, rewarding careful stall tactics and sacrificial setups.

The Defect

The automaton returns, its core logic rewritten. Orbs now chain in sequence, and channeling the right element at the right moment can trigger recursive passive havoc.
