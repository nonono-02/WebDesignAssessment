Roman Classic

Cacio e Pepe

A Roman pasta dish that translates to "cheese and pepper." The simple dish consists of spaghetti, black pepper, and Pecorino Romano.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Pasta (Bucatini works well)

Time to Make: 15 mins.

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and generously season with salt. Add pasta and cook until al dente.
  2. Reserve some pasta water and cool slightly.
  3. Drain pasta in a colander.
  4. Place skillet (or same pot used to cook pasta) over medium heat and add pasta back into pot.
  5. Add reserved pasta water and butter.
  6. Add grated cheese and black pepper and continue to toss together until the sauce comes together and is smooth and creamy.
  7. Top with more grated cheese and serve.
